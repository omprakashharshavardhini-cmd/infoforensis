What is Forensic Science?

Forensic science is the application of scientific principles and techniques to investigate crimes. It helps investigators analyze physical evidence such as fingerprints, blood samples, DNA, toxic substances, and digital data.

Forensic experts assist law enforcement agencies in solving crimes and presenting scientific evidence in courts of law.

About Infoforensis

Infoforensis is an educational platform created for students who are interested in forensic science. The website provides learning materials, project ideas, previous question papers, and helpful resources for students.

Our goal is to make forensic science learning easier by providing organized notes, useful videos, and problem-solving resources.
